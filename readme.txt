== Installation ==

1. Upload the `ics-snow` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` menu in WordPress.
1. Navigate to the `iCS-Snow` panel to customize options.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How can I enable/disable the falling snow? =
1. Log into your WP Dashboard, and go to the `iCS-Snow Options Panel`.
1. Select your customization option for the falling snow effect.
1. That's it!

= How can I make money promoting iCaughtSanta.com? =
1. Signup for our affiliate program here: http://www.catchacharacter.com/affiliates/
1. Log into your WP Dashboard, and go to the `iCS-Snow Options Panel`.
1. Enter your affiliate ID# in the field provided.
1. That's it!

= How can I enable/disable the peel advertisement? =
1. Log into your WP Dashboard, and go to the `iCS-Snow Options Panel`.
1. Select your customization option for the peel advertisement.
1. That's it!
